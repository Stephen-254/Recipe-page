Simple omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cookesd to perfection, optionally filled with choice of cheese, vegetable, or meat.

Preparation Time
  • Total: Approximately
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes
Ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat, herbs
Instructions
  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffy texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cooking the omeletter: Once the butter is melted and bubbly, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coats the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edge but are still slightly running in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
